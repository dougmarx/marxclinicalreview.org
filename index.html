About

Marx Clinical Review is led by Douglas Marx, DO, a board-certified Family Medicine physician with over 25 years of clinical and executive leadership experience. His work spans primary care, utilization management, peer review, and independent medical evaluation across ambulatory and acute care settings.

Dr. Marx has directed quality oversight for more than 500 physicians, delivering high-volume, guideline-driven clinical determinations with precision, consistency, and rapid turnaround. His approach emphasizes clear, defensible documentation aligned with established standards including ODG, MCG, and AMA Guides.
